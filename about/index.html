About Us

Since opened in 1997, La Parisian has become one of the most original and welcoming experiences in Irish-Franco food.


What makes us special?

There is our cooking, of course – our fresh Waterford produce, ever-changing menus and mouth-watering focus on flavour. There are cosy chats in the wine bar, warm and welcoming service from expert staff, a bright and buzzy atmosphere at the tables

There is our unique location in the seaside town of Dungarvan, a stone’s throw from the Copper Coast and Comeragh Mountains and the new Waterford Greenway . But most of all, there is La Parisians knack for serving up that most important of ingredients: a great time.

To add to all of this, we can also teach you our cookery techniques and our secrets in our chic, naturally-lit series of rooms custom-designed to cater for up to 14 participants at practical classes, or 30 at cookery demos in our cookery school.

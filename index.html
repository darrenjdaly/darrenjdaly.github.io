La Parisian is a reknowned Restaurant and cookery school
that strives to producean unrivalled experience for all
our customers (Food Critics)

Since our humble beginnings in 1997, we have relied on
quality and ensuring that everyone feels special and
bringing a special piece of Paris to Ireland.


Our Team is made up of:

  • Head Chef Laurant Hermann
    • Chef Hermann is a Parisian that was trained in some
      of the best schools in France. In the past few years,
      Chef Hermann has been the cornerstone of La Parisian
      Restaurant and frequently appears in our cookery school
  • Sous Chef Franco Evreux
    • Chef Evreux is again a French trained chef who specialises
      in cuisine from Normandy Chef Evreux frequently teaches
      in our cookery school when he is not ensuring the
      kitchen is running like a finely oiled machine
  • Sous Chef Fintin O'Connor
    • Chef O'Connor is our local connection
      He is charged with finding the best local produce and to
      tailor the french chef recipes to the Irish Palate.
